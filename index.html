About us

Terasave is designed for maximum accessibility and flexibility. You can start your savings journey with a minimum contribution of just ₦1,500. We offer flexible savings plans that allow you to choose your contribution frequency—daily, weekly, or monthly—to perfectly suit your personal financial rhythm and goals.

Terasave offers attractive interest rates on your savings. Our model allows users to earn a significant ₦40,000 interest for every ₦136,500 saved in your account. This goal is attainable through consistent contributions over time, without the pressure of requiring a large, one-time lump sum deposit.

The Food Commerce platform is Terasave's unique e-commerce feature that allows you to purchase food directly from the farm. This eliminates the need for middlemen who often spike the cost of food. By purchasing what you need at the farmer's price, you gain instant access to affordable, fresh food, helping you reduce your household expenses and combat the rising cost of basic necessities.
